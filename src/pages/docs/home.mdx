## Why Zaidan?

We love SolidJS. Its fine-grained reactivity and simple mental model make it a joy to work with. Thus, in order to build polished and accessible UIs, we need a library of ready-to-use components that feel as professional as Shadcn/ui.

That's why we created Zaidan (which means "Foundation" in Japanese).
